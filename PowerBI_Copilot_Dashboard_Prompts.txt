================================================================================
                         GLOBAL SLICERS (All Pages)
================================================================================

Add these slicers to each page for consistent filtering:

SLICER 1: Year
- Field: GIFT_TRAN[GIFT_DTE] (extract Year)
- Type: Dropdown or List
- Purpose: Filter by calendar year

SLICER 2: Campaign
- Field: CAMPAIGN[CAMPAIGN_DESC]
- Type: Dropdown
- Purpose: Filter by campaign

SLICER 3: Fund/Designation
- Field: GIFT_CATEGORY[GIFT_CAT_DESC]
- Type: Dropdown
- Purpose: Filter by fund

SLICER 4: Gift Type
- Field: GIFT_TRAN[GIFT_CLASS]
- Type: List (C=Cash, P=Pledge, M=Match, S=Soft Credit, N=Non-Cash)
- Purpose: Filter by gift classification
